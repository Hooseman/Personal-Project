REMINGTON RIFLES

Building on a legacy of excellence forged by Eliphalet Remington in 1816, Remington rifles have defined and defended the American way of life for more than 200 years.

The Model 700® bolt-action’s accuracy and dependability remain legendary more than 50 years since it was unveiled. And Remington innovation continues to enhance the capabilities of the Modern Sporting Rifle.

Remington modern rimfires lead the way in accuracy and reliability, while our classic pump-action and autoloaders deliver timeless lines and precision of the highest level.

And our pump-action Model 7600™ provides the fast follow-ups and pointability, should you need another shot at that shot of a lifetime.

RESEARCH & DEVELOPMENT

With nearly 200 years of history, Remington doesn’t just build firearms: we look to our past, assess the present and forecast the future. Our engineers at “The Rock,” our world-class skunk works and engineering center, devote thousands of hours to meticulously designing, testing and building the best products in the industry.

They are engineers, ballistic scientists, chemists, retired U.S. Military and law-enforcement experts, top-ranking competitive shooters, hunters and all manners of experts in the field. Together, they continue to revolutionize every facet of firearm and ammunition performance. From the world’s most advanced R&D testing facilities. The Rock, by Remington®.

Remington R&D masterminds engineer excellence for every passion and continue to carry the torch of innovation and reliability farther every year. The tack-driving Model 783 family continues to expand with new offerings, while the Model 700’s legend grows with each new configuration. And with the VersaPort gas-operated system, the unstoppable Versa Max and the new V3 continue to take the shotgun world by storm.

There is no such place as off the clock for Remington engineers, even when it comes to tried-and-true loads like the Remington Core-Lokt, celebrating over 75 years as The Deadliest Mushroom in the Woods. It was born in 1939 as the very first controlled expansion bullet, and we’ve been improving it ever since – a commitment to performance that’s made it the world’s best-selling centerfire hunting ammunition.

HyperSonic Rifle Boded. Hog Hammer. Ultimate Defense Handgun. Each exemplifies our massive, ongoing commitment to R&D. And in each line you’ll find more offerings every year. Because at Remington the innovation never stops.
