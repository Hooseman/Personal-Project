REMINGTON PUMP-ACTION SHOTGUNS

Heralded as the most innovative pump-action ever conceived, the Remington Model 870® pump-action shotgun changed the face of shotgun design like nothing ever before. With a receiver machined from a solid block of American steel, it’s the smoothest, most reliable pump-action shotgun in the market.

This superior dependability, as well as our ever-growing family, is why the Model 870® has been trusted by hunters, shooters and law-enforcement professionals above all others – six decades running. It is a proud legacy, measured in 12 million sold and countless generations served.
